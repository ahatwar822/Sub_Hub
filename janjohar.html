Hambirrao Mohite was the chief military commander of Chattrapati Shivaji's army. 
An able military general, he executed several campaigns for Chattrapati Shivaji Maharaj and later served under Chattrapati Sambhaji Maharaj. 

Born: 1632, Talbid
Died: 16 December 1687, Wai
Children: Tarabai
Siblings: Soyarabai
Grandchild: Shivaji II
Nephew: Rajaram I
Great grandchild: Rajaram II of Satara
