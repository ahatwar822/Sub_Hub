Pawankhind

Descripion


On a dreadful night in 1660, 
Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj finds himself cornered in the Panhala fort by the Bijapur Sultanate. 
However, the Maratha commanders risk their lives to save their leader.

Release date: 18 February 2022 (India)
Director: Digpal Lanjekar
Producers: Ajay Arekar, Aniruddha Arekar, Bhausaheb Arekar
Music director: Devdutta Baji
Language: Marathi
