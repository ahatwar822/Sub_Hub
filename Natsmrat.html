Natsmrat

Descripion


After retirement, Ganpat Belwalkar, a Shakespearean actor, divides his property amongst his two children. 
However, their ungratefulness leaves Ganpat and his wife homeless during their old age.

Release date: 1 January 2016 (India)
Director: Mahesh Manjrekar
Screenplay: Mahesh Manjrekar, Abhijeet Deshpande, Kiran Yadnyopavit
Story by: Kusumagraj
Language: Marathi
Cinematography: V. Ajith Reddy, Ajith V. Reddy
